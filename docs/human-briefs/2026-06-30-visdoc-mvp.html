VisDoc-Retrieve MVP diagnostic smoke brief

Current status / 当前状态

applying; evidence is diagnostic smoke only and not final benchmark evidence.

Inputs and candidate universe / 输入与候选集合

  • Pages: data/synthetic-smoke/pages.jsonl
  • Queries/qrels: data/synthetic-smoke/queries.jsonl
  • Evaluated split: dev
  • Candidate universe: evaluated_split_pages

Baselines / 方法

  • bm25: deterministic text baseline.
  • lexical_cosine: deterministic local lexical cosine baseline, not external dense embedding.
  • bm25_lexical_rrf: deterministic BM25 + lexical cosine RRF.
  • mock_visual: deterministic mock scaffold with MaxSim; not real ColPali or ColQwen execution.
